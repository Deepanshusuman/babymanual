# Daily Routines

Here is a sample routine for a newborn (0–3 months):

**Morning**
- 7:00 AM – Wake up & diaper change
- 7:30 AM – Feeding
- 8:00 AM – Tummy time / play
- 9:00 AM – Nap

**Afternoon**
- 12:00 PM – Feeding
- 12:30 PM – Diaper change
- 1:00 PM – Nap

**Evening**
- 4:00 PM – Feeding
- 4:30 PM – Play / fresh air
- 6:00 PM – Nap

**Night**
- 8:00 PM – Feeding & bedtime
- 11:00 PM – Dream feed
